Pre-Requisites

This class has no formal prerequisites, however students who have not completed CS2150 may benefit the most from this course. No computer science skills or knowledge is required to have fun and do well in this class.

Materials

There are no required materials to be purchased, and no textbook will be required. Any readings, assignments or other activities will be made available to students on the class website.

Grading

The grades for this class will be distributed as follows:
Attendance: 50%
Homeworks: 25%
Activities (in class): 25%
The class will use the standard 10-point grading scale.
If you have any grading concerns, please reach out to one of the instructors.
